The seven traditional visible spectrum colors, plus black and grey, are shown above with their hex color codes.

Note: The color named "violet" in CSS- hex code #ee82ee- is technically closer to a pale magenta. The violet shown above is what the human eye sees in rainbows. This is why color coordinate systems are so useful in CSS.
